About Us

At Pojok cafe in Braga, Bandung, Indonesia. Located in a quiet corner of the city, Pojok is a cafe that offers a warm and intimate atmosphere for anyone who wants to take a break from the hustle and bustle of daily activities.

Our Menu

  • Iced Americano

    An Americano is a coffee drink made from one or two shots of espresso mixed with hot water.

  • Hot Latte

    A latte is a coffee drink consisting of espresso and steamed milk, with a thin layer of foam on top.

  • Iced Mocha

    A mild, creamy flavor with a hint of bitterness from the coffee balanced with the natural sweetness of milk.

  • Cheese Cake

    A soft cake with a creamy taste and a slight sourness characteristic of cream cheese.

  • Mille Crepes

    Mille crepes is a cake made from thin layers of crepes stacked on top of each other and filled with cream between each layer.

  • Cookies

    Cookies are crispy on the outside but chewy on the inside with chocolate chips sprinkled on top.
